Description:
This dashboard provides key HR metrics, offering insights into workforce composition, retention, and employee satisfaction.

Key Metrics:

Retrenchment Rate – Number of employees laid off.
Active Workers – Total number of employees currently employed.
Satisfaction Level – Employee satisfaction scores.
Service Years – Employee tenure in years.
Job Roles – Distribution of employees across different roles.
Gender Distribution – Male vs. Female workforce percentage.
Due for Promotion – Employees eligible for promotion based on criteria.
Features:

Interactive filters to analyze workforce data across different dimensions.
Visualizations to highlight trends and employee demographics.
Usage:

Ideal for HR teams to track workforce dynamics, plan promotions, and analyze retention.
