Description:
This dashboard provides a comprehensive 1-page summary of key business performance metrics, replacing multiple separate reports with a single interactive view.

Key Metrics:

Gross Profit – Total earnings after deducting costs.
Quantity Sold – Number of units sold.
Sales Revenue – Total revenue generated.
Features:

Button Slicer – Allows users to toggle between Gross Profit, Quantity, and Sales within the same dashboard, reducing the need for multiple dashboards.
Interactive visuals for deeper insights.
Usage:

Designed for business stakeholders to quickly assess performance and make data-driven decisions.
