Description:
This dashboard provides a regional breakdown of revenue performance, helping stakeholders analyze financial trends across different areas.

Key Metrics:

Total Revenue – Overall earnings across all regions.
Revenue by Region – Breakdown of revenue by specific geographical areas.
Growth Trends – Year-over-year (YoY) and month-over-month (MoM) revenue changes.
Top & Bottom Performing Regions – Regions contributing the most and least to revenue.
Features:

Interactive filters to compare revenue performance across regions.
Trend analysis to identify growth opportunities and potential declines.
Dynamic visuals to present revenue insights clearly.
Usage:

Ideal for finance and sales teams to track regional performance, identify high-growth areas, and adjust business strategies accordingly.
